How the Study is Structured

The study has four short stages, in this order:

Practice (warm-up) → Calibration round → Work round 1 → Work round 2
  • Practice: a short warm-up so you can get used to the counting task. It does not affect your pay.
  • Calibration round: you complete the counting task once. The employer uses your performance here to set a starting salary offer (which does contribute to your pay). You do face the possibility of being rejected, getting 0 salary this period, if your performance is too low or your employer evaluates you unfavorably. The bar to clear here is lower than in the work rounds, however, since this round is just to figure out where you start.
  • Work rounds (2 total): in each one, you first decide how much to invest in training, then complete the counting task, then see your salary offer (always facing the possibility of rejection). Both work rounds count toward your bonus.
The Counting Task

You will see a target digit and a grid of numbers. Count how many times the target digit appears, type the number, and click Submit (or press the Enter key). You get instant feedback (✅ or ❌) and a new problem appears. You answer as many problems as you can in the time allowed.

Investing in Training (Work Rounds Only)

Before each work round, you may pay for training. Training does not directly boost your wage offer. Instead, it makes the task easier by shrinking the counting grid, so you can answer more problems in the same amount of time — which raises your performance and therefore your potential offer.

How You Get Paid
  • You start with a ${{ token_endowment_usd }} training allowance. Training tokens are paid for out of this allowance plus any salary you have earned — whatever you don't spend is yours to keep.
  • Each round's net earnings = salary − training cost.
  • Your final total = ${{ token_endowment_usd }} allowance + your net salary earnings.
  • All earnings are shown directly in dollars throughout the study.
Quick recap of the flow:
  1. Practice round (warm-up, no pay).
  2. Calibration round (counting task → starting salary offer).
  3. Work round 1: pick training tokens → counting task → see your salary.
  4. Work round 2: same as work round 1.
  5. Final summary and a short exit survey.
