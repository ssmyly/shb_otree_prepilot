A Research Study of Worker Behavior

Conducted by researchers at Emory University

What You Will Do

  • You will be playing the role of a worker
  • Read the instructions and complete a short practice round (warm-up, no pay)
  • Complete a calibration round followed by 2 work rounds of a short performance task
  • Before each work round, decide how much to invest in training, considering how your employer assesses you and your performance
  • Earn money based on your performance and investment decisions
  • Answer a brief exit survey (~5 minutes)

Estimated total time: 15–25 minutes

Informed Consent

Study Purpose: This study examines worker behavior in the labor market.

Procedures: You will complete a real-effort task (counting numbers in grids) — one calibration round followed by two work rounds — and make training-investment decisions before each work round. Your earnings depend on your performance and choices.

No deception: This study does not use deception.

Voluntary participation: Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may exit at any time and will be paid for the rounds you completed.

Data privacy: Your data will be stored securely and anonymized before analysis. Only your Prolific ID is collected; no other personally identifying information is retained.
