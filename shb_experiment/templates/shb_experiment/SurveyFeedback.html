Exit Survey (2 of 2) — Experiment Feedback

Debriefing Statement

Thank you for participating. This study examines how the availability of salary history information affects workers' decisions to invest in human capital (i.e., training). In some sessions, employers could see a worker's full salary history when making a wage offer ("no ban" condition); in others, that information was hidden ("salary history ban" condition). We are interested in whether salary history bans change the incentive to invest in productivity-enhancing training.

The information you provided will be used solely for academic research and will be kept confidential. Results will be reported only in aggregate form.

If you have questions about the study, please contact the research team. We appreciate your time and participation.
