Informed Consent

Study Purpose: This study examines how workers make decisions about skill development and investment in their human capital under different labor market conditions.

Procedures: You will complete a real-effort task (counting numbers in grids) and make investment decisions over 3 rounds. Your earnings depend on your performance and choices.

No deception: This study does not use deception. The wage formula you face will be fully explained before the task begins and shown to you throughout.

Voluntary participation: Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may exit at any time and will be paid for the rounds you completed.

Data privacy: Your data will be stored securely and anonymized before analysis. Only your Prolific ID is collected; no other personally identifying information is retained.
